Who We Are

We are Kaylla Smith and Pat Blackwell the owners of CityScapes Landscape Services.

We have worked at CityScapes Landscape Services for the last 13 years as the Maintenance Manager and the Office Manager before purchasing the company from the original owner.

We pride ourselves on our family atmosphere, integrity, and honest straight forward pricing.

CityScapes Landscape Services has been in business since 1990. We offer a full range of landscape design, installation and maintenance services to our residential and commercial customers in the metropolitan Oklahoma City area. We pride ourselves in working with our customers to develop practical and innovative solutions to everyday landscape problems, and at an affordable price.
